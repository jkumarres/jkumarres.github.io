Jaya Kumar. A

I am a research associate at the Indian Institute of Science, with a Ph.D. from Raman Research Institute, in Soft-Condensed Matter and a Master's degree in Electrical Engineering from Indian Institute of Science. Since October 2019 I have been a member of PTG (Plasma Theory Group), working on Gyro-Kinetic Code interfacing and machine-learning applications.
